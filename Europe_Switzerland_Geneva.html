Welcome to Geneva


Sleek, slick and cosmopolitan, Geneva is a rare breed of city: it's among Europe's priciest, its people chatter in every language and it's constantly perceived as the Swiss capital (it isn't). Superbly strung around the shores of Europe's largest Alpine lake, Switzerland's second-largest city has only half the population of Zurich; yet the whole world is here: 200-odd governmental and nongovernmental international organisations fill Geneva's plush hotels, feast on its international cuisine and help prop up its famed overload of banks, luxury jewellers and chocolate shops.
